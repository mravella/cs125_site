Welcome to CS125

In this course you will be introduced to the process of making a short computer animated film from beginning to end. It's super fun and usually a lot of work. To learn more about the course, click on Course Info. Also check out the CS125 FAQs.

To take this course, you must attend the first meeting on Wednesday, September 3, 2014 at 12 noon in Lubrano, 4th floor CIT.

If after the first class you want to take the course, you should fill out an application. Applications are sent via email to those students who attend the first class and are due the next morning by 9am. If there are more than 20 qualified students, I select based on class seniority, experience, enthusiasm, and how this course fits in with your plans. The class list will be available 24 hours later at 9am Friday Sept 5, and only those admitted will receive written permission to take the class.

Prerequisites

There are no fixed prerequisites for the class, but you must have some in-depth background in either computer graphics or in some aspect of visual art. If you have an online portfolio or website, you can provide a link in your application. This is not necessary, but can give us a better picture of you. Hope to see you at the first class! In the mean time, you can read about the course in the missive and syllabus.
